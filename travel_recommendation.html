Nestled in Vietnam’s Central Highlands, Dalat is a charming city known for its cool climate, beautiful flower gardens, French colonial architecture, and rolling pine-covered hills. Often called the “City of Eternal Spring,” Dalat offers a refreshing escape from the tropical heat of the lowlands.

Whether you are exploring the vibrant local markets, wandering around Xuan Huong Lake, visiting colorful pagodas, or discovering breathtaking waterfalls, Dalat promises a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure.
